Who We Are

We provide diverse financial services.

Gel Investments Ltd is a multi-purpose financial services company that provides funding solutions to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across a range of business sectors. Established in 2018, the Company's Head Office is located in England.

We aim to be the number one asset finance partner, providing funding to SMEs and commercial enterprises, enabling them to grow their businesses. We provide financial leverage to registered commercial entities operating in the real estate, oil and gas, manufacturing, trading, contracting, services, and logistics sectors.

Our Goal

We strive to become the leading loan and investment company in Europe and the Middle East, North America, by providing our customers with value-added services.
